What is Lifetopia World

Lifetopia World is a cozy life simulation and social sandbox game where players can build their own lifestyle through farming, fishing, exploration, progression, and creative living.

The world is designed to feel warm, playful, and inviting while creating a strong long term foundation for premium utility and future web3 expansion.
